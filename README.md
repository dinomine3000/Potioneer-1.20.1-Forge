# Potioneer Mod for Minecraft 1.20.1
This repo is the repo for my mod for minecraft Potioneer, a mod for minecraft in part based on the Web Novel Lord of Mysteries.
It was designed from the ground up to be Minecraft first, and a LOTM mod second. It includes unique and brand new pathways and adaptations of the series mechanics and powers to Minecraft.

This is my first mod for minecraft, so version control and a lot of finer details are lackluster.
I develop it sporadically, every now and again, but developement progress will always be public here. I plan to first develop the mod until I'm satisfied before thinking of porting to other versions. If I manage to get a team, that would help that along.

## Current plans
Development currently is focused on developing the powers of the 5 in-game pathways up to sequence level 5 (so levels 9 to 5, but nothing about 4-1), to reach a point of development good enough for Demo 2.
Demo 2 will be a large scale debugging and feedback server with plenty of people to assess and improve the mod.

## What's in the mod now
Mechanics that have been implemented but not completed (I've got the base mechanisms done but it doesn't have much content or is incomplete):
 - Potion concotion system;
 - Acting progress;
 - Multi-pathway beyonders and interchangeable pathways;
 - Artifacts and characteristics;
 - Divination;
 - Rituals
 - Charm creation and customizable recipes
 - Gods and Religion, as in, being able to pray to a God.
 - Documentation book (based on collecting individual pages through exploration);
 - Luck system (leveraged by the Wheel of Fortune pathway);
 - Some entities;
 - HUD customization;
 - Ally groups and parties;
 - Ability hotbar and quick select, including Intrinsic and Artifact abilities;
 - Advancement mini-game;
 - Churches for each pathway's "religion"
 - Restrict spirituality amount the further one descends the sequences;
 - Advancement ritual system (basic);
 - Dropping characteristics on death and sanity system;
 - Extensive config files;

## Roadplan for Demo 2
This is, currently, divided into 3 phases.
### 1. Initial Pathway Development:
This pertains only to the initial development of abilities, without taking into account complexity, game balance or fun.
 - ~~Wheel of Fortune Pathway~~
 - Tyrant Pathway (WiP, currently, sequences 9 and 8 are fully complete, sequence 7 is ongoing, 6 and 5 have not started)
 - Mystery Pathway
 - Red Priest Pathway
 - Paragon Pathway
### 2. Pathway tweaking and reworking
This is based on a simple testing session between close people to assess complexity, game balance and fun that each player gets
This will only begin once phase 1 is complete.

### 3. Debugging and refining
Probably quickest phase. Just encompasses fixing bugs, tweaking effects, and adding any lacking features for demo 2 (like missing documentation pages, charm recipes, etc...)

## Demo 2?
Demo 2 is what I call the large scale testing that will take place once I'm satisfied with the current mod development. It'll serve to advertise the mod and stress test its performance, as well as just having a bunch of people having fun.
It'll consist of a server open 24/7 for about a month at most with almost complete freedom between people. Further details have been *concealed*.
